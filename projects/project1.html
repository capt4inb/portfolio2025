LINKTO Platform

A Commercial Solution for Influencer Marketing

Timeline

11/2024 - 03/2025

Role

UX/UI Designer

Technologies

Figma Adobe XD Prototyping

Project Overview

LINKTO is a revolutionary platform designed to empower influencers in monetizing their digital presence effectively. The platform serves as a bridge between content creators and their audience, providing tools for content management, analytics, and monetization.

Challenge

The main challenge was to create an intuitive interface that could handle complex functionalities while remaining accessible to users with varying levels of technical expertise. We needed to balance feature-rich capabilities with user-friendly design.

Solution

We implemented a clean, modern design with a focus on user experience. Key features include:

  • Intuitive dashboard for content management
  • Real-time analytics and performance metrics
  • Integrated monetization tools
  • Customizable profile pages
  • Mobile-responsive design

Results

The platform successfully launched with positive user feedback, particularly regarding its ease of use and comprehensive feature set. Initial user testing showed a 95% satisfaction rate among content creators.
